Privacy Policy for GUPI24

At GUPI24, accessible from https://gupi24.vercel.app, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by GUPI24 and how we use it.

Log Files

GUPI24 follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, GUPI24 uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to www.website.com and other sites on the internet.
